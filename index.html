About GhostLight Research

I’m Sarah Rogue, an information broker with a soft spot for quiet clarity, emo aesthetics, and humane technology. I specialize in helping people who feel overwhelmed by tools, options, and data.

As an INFP‑T, I bring deep listening, careful research, and a gentle but precise way of turning chaos into structure. GhostLight Research is where empathy meets analysis—where we light up the patterns hiding in the dark.

Core values

  • Ethical: No shady data, ever.
  • Human‑centered: Tools should serve people, not the other way around.
  • Quietly powerful: Low‑drama, high‑clarity research.
  • Accessible: Explanations that actually make sense.
